What’s on the horizon for the Growth Team?

Systems, technologies and skill insights that have transformed careers.
The very core of our group’s purpose is to provide the best possible environment for all of our members to thrive. This environment is created by positioning our customers at the centre of everything, and to drive improvement in customer experience through genuine leadership and technological innovation.

The Ray White Corporate team keeps ahead of the trend as industry leaders by providing tools so our salespeople and administration teams can be more efficient. One System, with its range of CRM, reporting and document management capabilities, gives our salespeople and admin teams the means to use time effectively, so our clients can be our number one focus.

With a focus on data, insight and automation.

We are here to build bespoke data products that extend the competitive advantage of our network.

With the One System suite as our foundation, we integrate and tie together our network’s greatest technical advantage — our group data — into reporting and automation tools that help our businesses achieve better decision making and greater customer service.

Ray White develops specialised software to solve these common issues: consumer facing apps used by Agents and Principals to deliver efficient customer service, back-of-house systems to support scaled franchisor processes, and data science products to enable competitive planning and decision making for all three.
